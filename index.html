Biography

Nicolas Brochec is a researcher and composer in computer music specializing in real-time recognition of instrumental playing techniques. He received his Ph.D. from the Tokyo University of the Arts as a recipient of the Japanese Government (MEXT) Scholarship. He is currently an associate researcher at IRCAM, Music Representations Team, REACH research group.

Education

  • Ph.D. in Music Creativity and Environment (2023–2026) — Tokyo University of the Arts
  • M.A. in Music Composition — Académie Supérieure de Musique de Strasbourg
  • M.A. in Computer Music — Université Paris 8
  • B.A. in Musicology — Université Paris 8
  • H.N.D. in Audio Engineering — Pôle Image et Son Biarritz
